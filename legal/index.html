MiyavMap

Legal and safety pages for the MiyavMap Android app.

MiyavMap is a community app for photographing real street cats, collecting them as trading cards on a shared map, and helping identify and look after them (the Street Cat Society).

Pages

  • Privacy Policy — what data the app collects, why, and your rights.
  • Terms of Service — the rules for using MiyavMap.
  • Account & Data Deletion — how to delete your account and data, with or without the app installed.
  • Child Safety Standards — our standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).
MiyavMap — an independent, free, ad-free app. These pages are the hosted copies of the documents also shown inside the app.

Account & Data Deletion

For the MiyavMap Android app (package it.catchcat.app), developed by an independent developer.

You can delete your MiyavMap account and its data at any time — from inside the app, or by request if you no longer have the app installed. Deletion is free and does not require any justification.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (immediate)

  1. Open MiyavMap and sign in with the Google account you used for MiyavMap.
  2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Deletion runs immediately on your device — no waiting period.

If you have uninstalled the app but still have your Google account, you can reinstall it, sign back in, and use the same path.

Option 2 — Request deletion without the app

If you can't or don't want to reinstall the app, email albybit31@gmail.com with the subject "MiyavMap account deletion request", sending the email from the Google account address you used to sign in to MiyavMap (this is how we verify the account is yours), and include your MiyavMap nickname if you remember it. We will confirm and complete the deletion within 30 days, usually much sooner.

If you only ever used MiyavMap anonymously (never signed in with Google), no account or email exists on our side to identify you by; anonymous sessions hold no personal information beyond the content you chose to post publicly.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (Google sign-in link, email, display name).
  • Your profile: nickname, avatar photo, stats, helper roles, and your reserved username.
  • All the cards you captured, including their photos and locations.

What is kept, and for how long

Some community content that other users' collections and cat profiles depend on stays visible with your name removed: votes you cast on others' cards, cat updates you posted, and cat profiles you were the first to register for others to follow. This reflects a technical constraint of the shared map, not a choice to retain your identity — nothing identifying you remains attached to that content. Abuse reports you filed may be retained as moderation records; you can ask for those to be deleted too (see the Privacy Policy, "Reports and moderation").

Beyond those exceptions, deleted data is removed immediately and is not kept for any additional retention period.

Questions

Anything unclear, or a deletion that doesn't seem to have worked: email albybit31@gmail.com. Your data-protection rights (access, rectification, portability and more) are described in the Privacy Policy.

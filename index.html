How it works

Catch

Photograph a street cat and your photo becomes a trading card — Common, Rare, Epic or Legendary, each with its own frame.

Know

Every real cat gets a shared identity page the neighborhood maintains together: welfare profile, ear-tip and TNR status, sighting updates.

Help

Volunteers recognize and care for the cats they see every day. A needs-attention flag means someone should go check on that cat.
